ADDA

Architecture Project

A design study exploring community spaces and social gathering architecture. Adda investigates how spatial design can facilitate meaningful human interaction and cultural exchange.

Project Type

Community Center

Tools & Software

Rhino 3D, Revit, D5 Render

Year

2024

Focus

Social Interaction

Project Overview

Adda is a study into community spaces that facilitate social exchange through careful spatial organisation and material choice. The design focuses on flexible public areas that support diverse activities and interactions.

Parametric exploration allowed multiple layout configurations to be evaluated for circulation and sociability, refining the final spatial strategies.
