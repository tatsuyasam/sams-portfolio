Community Building

Building an asset-based community through fostering interactions, creating connections, and encouraging empathy.

By offering multiple gathering spaces for both small and large groups and designing areas that encourage people to cross paths, a strong, connected community can be nurtured. The central area and MPH are designed to host events and accommodate large groups, serving as the heart of community activities. However, building a community where trust truly thrives among individuals is challenging .

Drawing inspiration from Skillseed, the first step is fostering empathy within the community. To achieve this, I introduced the Sharehub—a dedicated space where people can support one another by sharing skills and resources. For example, someone who needs help sewing clothes can seek assistance at the Sharehub, where materials and equipment are provided to encourage collaboration and mutual support within the migrant worker community.
