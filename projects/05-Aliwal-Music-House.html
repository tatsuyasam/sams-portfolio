ALIWAL MUSIC HOUSE

Architecture Project

A community music hub that lowers the barriers to making and experiencing music. Located in the heart of Aliwal Street's vibrant arts district.

Project Type

Cultural Center

Tools & Software

Rhino 3D, Grasshopper, D5 Render

Year

2025

Focus

Music & Communitites

Project Overview

The building complements its heritage of visual culture by introducing sound as a shared medium of memory and creativity. Designed in wood to resonate like an instrument, the Music House invites all — from seasoned musicians to curious beginners — to jam, learn, and explore in an informal, welcoming environment.

Through open jam sessions, archives of Malay musical heritage, workshops, and community gatherings, the Soundhouse strengthens Aliwalʼs cultural fabric by bringing people together through sound — celebrating the music of the past while inspiring new expressions for the future.
