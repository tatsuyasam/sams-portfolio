Architecture Project

Aliwal Music House

A cultural center designed to celebrate music and community. This project explores how architectural design can support creative expression and acoustic excellence in a shared space.

Project Type

Cultural Center

Tools & Software

Rhino 3D, Grasshopper

Year

2023

Focus

Acoustic Design

Project Overview

Aliwal Music House is conceived as a cultural centre that supports performance, rehearsal, and community-led events. The design balances acoustic performance with social functionality to create adaptable spaces for diverse activities.

Parametric studies informed the arrangement of performance halls and audience seating to optimise sound quality while preserving clear circulation and visual connections between spaces.
