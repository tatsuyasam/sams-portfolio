TOBARA PROJECT

Architecture Project

Tobara is a resort aiming to foster immersive cultural exchange to revive and sustain fading Batak craftsmanship through meaningful connections between travelers and local communities.

Project Type

Hospitality Resort

Tools & Software

Rhino 3D, Grasshopper, D5 Render

Year

2024

Focus

Craft Heritage

Crafted Escapes

Inspired by the rich traditions of Karonese culture, this crafted stay offers more than a retreat — it’s a week-long journey into heritage and making. From sourcing raw materials to producing your own tools, every step immerses you in the skills and wisdom passed down through generations.

Guided by local artisans, you’ll experience the artistry of Lake Toba firsthand. By the end of your stay, you’ll not only take home a crafted object but also a deeper connection to the culture, traditions, and timeless spirit that shaped it.
