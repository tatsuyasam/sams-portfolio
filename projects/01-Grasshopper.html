Projects Overview

This collection of explorations looks at how computational tools such as Grasshopper, TouchDesigner, Python, and Dynamo can be used as part of the design process. Rather than focusing on a single outcome, these experiments explore different ways of generating forms, manipulating data, creating unique graphics, and testing rendering styles through digital workflows.

Each exploration became a way to experiment, iterate, and discover unexpected results, showing how computational design can be used not just for architecture, but also for visual expression and creative problem-solving.
