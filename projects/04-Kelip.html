Architecture Project

Kelip

An innovative lighting and spatial design project exploring how luminosity and form interact. Kelip investigates parametric variations to create dynamic environments through light.

Project Type

Public Architecture

Tools & Software

Rhino 3D, Grasshopper, D5 Render

Year

2025

Focus

Light & Colour

Project Overview

Kelip investigates the interplay of light and form to produce immersive architectural experiences. Parametric strategies enable exploration of luminous patterns and spatial sequences that respond to user movement.

Design iterations focused on material translucency and responsive lighting strategies to shape atmospheres across interior and exterior spaces.
