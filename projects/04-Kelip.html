KELIP

Architecture Project

What if play could help us reimagine Singapore—not just as a city to live in, but one to truly connect across generations, time, and nature?

Project Type

Public Architecture

Tools & Software

Rhino 3D, Grasshopper, D5 Render

Year

2025

Focus

Light & Colour

Project Overview

Kelip is an intergenerational sensory playground for all ages, where movement, reflection, and stillness shape ever-changing visual and spatial experiences. Rooted in the belief that play is a dialogue between body, space, and time, it transforms simple materials—glass, mirrors, and water—into instruments of wonder

Instead of technology, Solari uses natural elements—sunlight, shadows, reflections, and caustics—to create dynamic spaces that shift with time, position, and interaction. From mirrored chambers that multiply the self, to sun-tracing shadow lines and rippling water casting light, each moment invites curiosity and awe. Solari isn't a static structure, but a living environment that reframes the everyday into something extraordinary.
